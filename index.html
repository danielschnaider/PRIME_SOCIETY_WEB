About PRIME Society

A formula for a poverty-free, sustainable future.

PRIME Society is a nonprofit federation that provides what we believe is the formula for a poverty-free and sustainable future — through cutting-edge tools, best practices, and open standards.

We are a global initiative dedicated to pioneering next-generation governance models designed to eradicate poverty through AGE-Zones policies (Autonomous Governance Experimental Zones).

The Backbone of Everything

Kairos — Where Civic Coordination Becomes Reality

Kairos is PRIME Society's flagship platform — a modular civic operating system that coordinates time, goods, capital, opportunities, trust and governance across 40 apps.

Everything succeeds if Kairos succeeds. It is the infrastructure layer that turns vision into measurable impact.

  • 🤝 Micro-Voluntarism — match skills to urgent civic tasks
  • 🌿 Food Rescue — AI-coordinated redistribution network
  • 💡 Impact Funding — outcome-based grants with trust scoring
  • 🛡 Hestia AI — intelligent automation with human oversight
