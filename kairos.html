PRIME Tools

What Kairos Enables

Kairos — from the Ancient Greek for the opportune, decisive moment — is the PRIME Society tool that helps leaders, communities and organizations identify when, where and how to act.

  • 🎯 Early identification of governance windows of opportunity.
  • 📊 Evidence-driven prioritization across competing initiatives.
  • ⚡ Faster, more confident decisions in complex, high-stakes environments.
  • 🗣 A common vocabulary for purpose, results and measurement across teams.

Flagship Apps

Three Apps. Proven Impact.

The first three Kairos apps are live and generating real impact — from volunteer matching to food rescue to impact-based funding.

01

Micro-Voluntarism

A micro-task platform that matches skilled volunteers to urgent civic needs in minutes. From translation to legal support, from design to data analysis — every hour of time donated is verified, tracked and counted as impact.

  • → 7,840 active volunteers globally
  • → 92,300 tasks completed
  • → 386 partner organizations
Pilot
02

Food Rescue

An AI-coordinated redistribution network that connects surplus food from businesses to NGOs, shelters and families in need — with real-time logistics, cold-chain tracking and verified delivery confirmations.

  • → 142,300 kg rescued
  • → ~284,600 meal equivalents
  • → 85% fulfillment rate
Pilot
03

Impact Funding

Outcome-based grant management with milestone verification, trust scoring and real-time disbursement. Funders see exactly where their money goes and track impact down to the individual beneficiary.

  • → $4.8M in pledged funding
  • → 38 funded projects
  • → 94% trust score on verified events
Pilot
Hestia AI

Intelligent Automation with Human Oversight

Hestia is the AI orchestration layer inside Kairos — automating routine coordination while keeping humans firmly in control of every consequential decision.

It monitors platform data, suggests actions, executes safe automations and alerts coordinators when human judgment is needed. Every agent action is logged, auditable and reversible.

  • 🤖 Automated task routing and volunteer matching
  • 🔔 Proactive alerts for urgent needs and expiring offers
  • 📋 Evidence collection and milestone verification
  • 🔒 Full audit trail — every action logged and auditable

How It Works

How Kairos Works

Kairos sits on top of the broader PRIME Framework. It combines the PRIME principles — Purpose, Results, Innovation, Measurement, Empirical — with practical playbooks for sensemaking, scenario design and rapid execution.

🎯

Define Purpose

Every app starts with a clear civic problem and a measurable success metric.

⚙️

Coordinate Resources

Kairos routes time, goods, capital and skills to where they're needed most.

🛡

Verify Impact

Every action generates a trust signal — verified by multiple parties and logged immutably.

📈

Improve Continuously

Hestia AI learns from outcomes and improves matching, routing and decision logic over time.
